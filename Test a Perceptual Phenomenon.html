Null Hypothsis, H0 - There is no change in time between two measuring congruent or incongruent task. Alternate Hypothesis, H1 - Incongruent task takes more time than congruent task. H0: i c (i - Population mean of incongruent values, c - Population mean of congruent values)

H1: i > c (i - Population mean of incongruent values, c - Population mean of congruent values)

Two tailed statistical test
